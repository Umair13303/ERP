if i force users to learn and enter i am gonna loose my sale worth no matter how strong i make it 
and the owners might want every kind of report plus you never trust your client on requirment they can ask for any thing so i did some backend & database driven things
1.we can not show complicated things like is chart of accounts in a production configuration form:
solution: in a database table i setted up default its which will help in controlling the form in much better way
2. some product specially in clothing sector has varients like color & size:
solution: i added a "," attributeIds in product form so either the user can choose what will be the attributes or the company for once can tell me their global attributes and i will set them in default setting table
and save with every product by hiding & not clearing it
3. i figured out if my product has attributed and as opening balance i also have to set prices so making a opening balance form was not suitable:
solution: made inventory adjsutment form , added a adjustment type: OPENING BALANCE in database and set its IsAutoPriceUpdate = true so in this case it will 
auto update the prices on setup in AFProductPriceLog
